**SYMPRO (Systematic Literature Management and Processing Tool)** is an end-to-end AI-powered platform for systematic reviews — covering search, screening, risk of bias, data extraction, and writing.

- 🔍 AI-powered literature search & PICOS generation  
- 📊 Smart screening & workflow management  
- ⚖️ Risk of Bias (RoB) analysis

## ⚖️ Risk of Bias Tools

- [SYMPRO-AI ROB Master](https://sympro.ai/tools#robmaster) — AI-powered platform for Risk of Bias by AccuScript 
- [RoB 2](https://www.riskofbias.info/welcome/rob-2-0-tool) — Cochrane tool for assessing risk of bias in randomized trials

- [Newcastle-Ottawa Scale (NOS)](http://www.ohri.ca/programs/clinical_epidemiology/oxford.asp) — Quality assessment scale for observational studies
